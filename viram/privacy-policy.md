**Last Updated: March 22, 2026**

Viram (referred to as "the App," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information.

## 1. No Data Collection
Viram is a completely offline application designed for digital wellbeing and screen time tracking.

- **Personal Information:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

- **Permissions:** The App may request permissions (e.g., Usage Access) to provide its core functionality. These permissions are used solely to process data locally on your device.

## 2. No Third-Party Sharing
Since we do not collect any data, we do not share, sell, or trade any user information with third parties.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
All information generated by the App is stored in your device's local storage. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data associated with Viram will be permanently deleted.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our App does not collect any information from children. Since no data is collected, we are inherently compliant with regulations like COPPA.
